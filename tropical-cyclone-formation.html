TROPICAL CYCLONE FORMATION

Date Issued: 13 October 2025
Valid: Until Models Change the Forecast

Forecast Summary:
Week-1 (October 13-19 2025)

  • Tropical Cyclone Formation #1 - a disturbance forming in the central Pacific Ocean could potentially evolve into a LPA or Tropical Depression, making its way enter the PAR on October 14 or 15. By October 15 or 16, it might dissipate to the east of Mindanao.

  • Meanwhile, Tropical Cyclone Formation #2 - a disturbance off the east coast of the Philippines, outside of the PAR, has a high chance of developing into a tropical cyclone or tropical depression. By October 16-17, it may enter the PAR as a Tropical Depression, and it could be given the local name RAMIL by DOST-PAGASA.

    • All the details and data are still subject to change, so everyone is advised to stay updated with the latest forecasts.

    Prepared by: HH/AT

    This Tropical Cyclone Formation was created by Cabuyao City Weather Information to help the public better understand any potential storms or weather disturbances entering the Philippine Area of Responsibility.

    We conduct this because our agency continuously monitors developing weather systems. We need our own forecast to keep track of possible tropical disturbances or weather systems that may enter the country.

    However, we do not recommend using our forecast as the official reference. We still highly recommend following the official forecasts issued by DOST-PAGASA.

    Our forecast is only generated by our Weather and Climate Team, using data from various weather models. This allows us to identify any tropical disturbances that may develop and affect not only the Philippines but also our monitored area here in the City of Cabuyao.
